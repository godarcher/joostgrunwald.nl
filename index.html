Student Assistant Radboud University

Metaphorical Neural Network

I wrote a Deep Neural Network which utilizes an XGB decision tree to create a metaphorical classifier. Deep learning based upon word embeddings, word context, object lemma and subject lemma to determine which words in a sentence are metaphorical.
I also made a nexislexis news scraper to scrape a dutch covid-19 news database of 41.153 articles, which was ran over by the neural network to generate data about metaphors and covid-19.

  • Scrape nexisuni database
  • Automatic metadata seperation
  • Neural Network deployment

Programmer Caprice Baksteen

2020-2020
02. -

Sand remover and stone straightener

I developed a python algorithm to find sand on stone pictures and automaticly remove chunks of sand found. So called blobs are seperated so we end up with fully ready brick pieces. We also developed an algorithm that detects angles of bricks to rotate them so they strand straight. All of this to prepare brick pictures for automatic wall generation.

Programmer GDS Keramiek

2020-2020
03. -

Cotton removal system

I created a system which extends upon the work I did by Caprice. This system detects cotton remains that are left sticking on the stone from the showroom and effectively erases those pixels. Stacked upon the previous system, we created a 3 step stone preperation system:

  • Sand and cotton removal
  • Fixing turned stone angles
  • Ready to generate stone panels

After this I did some back-end site work for Caprice and made stone panels in photoshop from pictures of approximatly 270 different walls, time in which I learned a lot about photo editing and photoshop.

In order to maximalize programming perfomance, all my work is spread over different development cycles, in which customer feedback is very important.

  • Draw & Define

    Cycle of discussion and initial design

    We want to determine the tasks that lay ahead and agree on an initial design of features, priorities and ideas.

  • Develop & Design
